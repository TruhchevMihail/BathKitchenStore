Shipping & Returns

Delivery Information

Perfect Home Studio works with trusted courier partners to ensure fast and secure delivery across Bulgaria and the EU.

  • Standard Delivery (BG): 1–3 business days.
  • EU Delivery: 3–7 business days depending on location.
  • Large / Heavy Products: May require 3–5 business days.
  • Delivery time is confirmed after order verification.

You will receive confirmation by phone or email before dispatch. Tracking details are provided once the order is shipped.

Shipping Costs

  • Free shipping on selected promotional products.
  • Standard courier rates apply for other products.
  • International shipping cost calculated at checkout.

Returns & Exchanges

You have the right to return unused products within 14 calendar days from the date of delivery.

  • Products must be unused and in original packaging.
  • All accessories, manuals and documentation must be included.
  • Return shipping cost is covered by the customer unless product is defective.

Tip: Always inspect your order upon delivery. If visible damage is present, notify the courier immediately.

Warranty

All products sold by Perfect Home Studio are covered by manufacturer warranty according to brand policies.

Warranty Coverage

  • Coverage period varies by brand and product category.
  • Warranty is valid only with invoice or receipt.
  • Defects in materials or manufacturing are covered.

Warranty Does Not Cover

  • Improper installation or misuse.
  • Normal wear and tear.
  • Damage caused by incorrect water pressure or maintenance.

For warranty claims, please contact us with:

  • Product name and code
  • Invoice number
  • Photos or video of the issue

Our team will assist you and coordinate directly with the manufacturer when needed.
